Origin

Born from
Wave Physics

Cymatics Studio Lab is a powerful real-time wave simulator and generative art tool inspired by two pioneers who made sound visible.

Explore cymatics patterns, Faraday waves, and mathematical visualizations through an interactive interface designed for both scientists and artists.

Michael Faraday
Discovered Faraday waves — standing wave patterns on vibrating liquid surfaces (1831).
Hans Jenny
Founded the field of cymatics — the study of visible sound and vibration patterns (1967).
