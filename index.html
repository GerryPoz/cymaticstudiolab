Cymatics Studio Lab is not an audio visualizer.
It is a controlled frequency environment where sound (0–100 Hz) generates structured physical resonance patterns.

01 — Core principle

Cymatics is the study of visible sound vibration. When a surface is exposed to controlled frequency energy, matter organizes into geometric structures.

0 – 100 Hz

This system focuses exclusively on low-frequency resonance, where physical vibration becomes visually observable.

02 — System behavior

Unlike traditional audio-to-visual tools, Cymatics Studio Lab does not interpret sound.

It directly applies frequency excitation to a simulated medium, producing stable or chaotic structural patterns depending on input.

03 — Frequency response

Lower frequencies (0–30 Hz): slow, large-scale wave structures.
Mid frequencies (30–70 Hz): complex geometric interference.
Higher range (70–100 Hz): dense, fast oscillation fields.
